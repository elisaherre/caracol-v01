About Us

Who We Are

At Caracol Studio, we believe in the power of design to transform spaces and enhance experiences. Founded by Sofia Ramirez and Alex Torres, our studio brings together a dynamic team of creative minds dedicated to pushing the boundaries of design innovation.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every space tells a story, evokes emotions, and fosters connection. From residential interiors to commercial environments, our goal is to create designs that inspire and resonate with people on a deeper level.

Our Approach

Combining artistry with functionality, we approach each project with a fresh perspective and a commitment to excellence. Our collaborative process ensures that every detail is meticulously crafted, from concept development to final execution.

Our Services

From interior design to architectural consulting, we offer a wide range of services to meet the unique needs of our clients. Whether you're looking to renovate your home, design a new office space, or create a custom furniture piece, we have the expertise and creativity to bring your vision to life.
